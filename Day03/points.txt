1. Introduction to Supervised Learning 

What is supervised learning?

Supervised Learning is a ML technique where the models are trained on datasets that contains inputs (features) and known outputs

Titanic Dataset
---------------
Age
Sex
Fare
SibSp
Parch

Output
------
Survived(0, 1)

Classification - discrete output var  -> result of interview = Pass / Fail

Regression - continuous output var  -> temperature -> 28 dc, 30 dc, 26dc


Classification
--------------
1. Email filtering => email is a spam or not_spam

2. Medical Diagnosis => predict whether the patient is diabetic or non diabetic

3. Credit Scoring => predict whether the loan applicant is high risk or low risk

4. Sentiment Analysis =>  predict whether the product review is positive, neutral or negative

5. Image Recognition => predict if the image is of a dog, cat or a bird


Regression
----------
House price prediction => Estimate the selling price of a house on features like size, location, and number of rooms

Stock Market Forecasting => Predict the future price of a stock given historical trends and market indicators

Weather forecasting => predict the temperature (a continuous value) based on the past weather

Sales forecasting => Estimate monthly sales revenue based on advertising spend, seasonality and market condition
